Welcome

Your Kitchen has everything covered for dinner tonight. Add all the ingredients from your pantry into our site and let Your Kitchen find the perfect recipe for you. Whenever you use an ingredient in a recipe, we'll automatically update your ingredients so we never suggest a recipe you can't make.

How to Get Started:

  • Click on "Your Ingredients" and add whatever is in your kitchen.
  • Click on "Recipes" to choose from the list of possible recipes you can make.
  • Decide on a recipe and click "Done" after making the recipe.
  • Come back when you're hungry again for an up-to-date selection of recipes.
